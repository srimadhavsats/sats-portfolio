// journey

14 years down the rabbit hole

Not a tourist. I was here before the ETFs, before DeFi summer, before the word "Web3" existed — and I've touched nearly everything the space has produced.

2012

First contact

Heard about Bitcoin from a gaming friend planning a mining rig. Went straight down the rabbit hole: halvings, block rewards, SHA-256, and the cypherpunk ideals behind censorship-resistant money. Explored TOR, SOCKS5 proxies, and open-source security.

2012–2013

First transactions & mining era

First on-chain BTC transaction. Earned sats through the legendary early faucets and task-to-earn platforms. Evaluated ASIC mining economics (Antminer S1 ROI models) and ran a Bitcoin node on home hardware.

2014–2016

Multi-coin & self-custody era

Accumulated and traded BTC, Litecoin, XRP and Dogecoin. Learned wallet architecture deeply — Electrum, Loaf, Toast, GateHub — and moved to non-custodial storage. Used LocalBitcoins, Paxful and ShapeShift when "just swap it" wasn't a thing yet.

2017

The first mania — and its lessons

Lived through the ICO bubble and fork wars (BTC/BCH). Experienced CEX counterparty risk firsthand — a lesson that turned into a permanent self-custody discipline.

2020

The deep study

Re-entered as institutions arrived (Saylor, Musk era) and used lockdowns to study Bitcoin exhaustively — monetary theory, cryptography, consensus, and everything in between. Interest became obsession.

2020–2023

Power user of everything

DeFi staking & LP farming, NFTs, play-to-earn, ICOs, airdrops (including the Terra domain-mint airdrop), perp DEXs, and cross-chain bridging across 10+ ecosystems. Built a 4-EMA + VWAP/MACD/RSI scalping system and executed ~1,000 futures trades. Explored DePIN, NOSTR, Lens and decentralized domains early.

2024

All-in on Web3

Left a 5+ year corporate QA/analyst career (Google hardware programs at GlobalLogic) to commit fully to the space. Ran incentivized nodes on VPS infrastructure, became admin of crypto node-running and Bitcoin communities, switched daily driver to Arch Linux.

2025–2026

Builder mode

Studying Solidity through Cyfrin Updraft and shipping real products: an AMM DEX in Solidity/Foundry, a macro crypto intelligence platform, a high-frequency market telemetry pipeline, and a real-time news command center.
